## What is NORCET?

NORCET stands for **Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test**. It is the exam conducted by AIIMS at a national level for recruiting Nursing Officers in all AIIMS hospitals and some other central government hospitals.

## Who gives it?
The eligible candidates for this exam are who those hold either of these

- B.Sc. Nursing Degree
- Post-Basic B.Sc (Done after GNM)
- GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery) + 2 years of hospital experience

## When NORCET Happens?
It happens twice a year. The months are usually April/May for first cycle and September/October for second cycle.

Each NORCET exam happens in two stages. First **Prelims Exam** and then **Mains Exam**. Only those candidates who are qualified in Prelims become eligible for the Main exam.

## How much Competition is in NORCET?
Competition in NORCET for getting a job as staff nurse or Nursing Officer in AIIMS institutes is decently high. In last exam which was NORCET 10 total 92026 candidates appeared. Out of which only 14,527 candidates were called for the stage II exam. And the total vacancies are 2,779 that are going to be allotted.

## Benefits of Preparing for NORCET?
The benefits are more than just get prepared for appearing in NORCET. It also get you prepared for other exams held for recruiting Nursing jobs like CHO and other state level exams
