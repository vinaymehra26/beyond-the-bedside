## What is NORCET?
For securing a job as a Staff Nurse at any prestigious institute of *AIIMS*, NORCET is the only path for all Nursing Graduates.

NORCET stands for **Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test**. It is the exam conducted by AIIMS at a national level for recruiting **Nursing Officers** in all AIIMS hospitals and some other central government hospitals.

---
## Who gives it?
The eligible candidates are those who completed either of...

- B.Sc. Nursing
- M.Sc. Nursing
- Post-Basic B.Sc (Done after GNM)
- GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery) + 2 years of hospital experience

---
## When NORCET Happens?
It can happen twice a year but it's actual timing depends on the schedule provided by AIIMs. The months are usually April/May for first cycle and September/October for second cycle. You can see the actual schedules here at official AIIMS notification section for [NORCET](https://aiimsexams.ac.in/landingpage/courses/advertisement/66ab7926ebb3815a4b707804).

Each NORCET exam happens in two stages. First **Prelims Exam** and then **Mains Exam**. Only those candidates who are qualified in Prelims become eligible for the Main exam.

---
## How much Competition is in NORCET?
Competition in NORCET for getting a job as staff nurse or Nursing Officer in AIIMS institutes is decently high. In the last exam which was NORCET 10 total 92026 candidates appeared in the Prelims. Out of which only 14,527 candidates were called for the stage II exam. And the total vacancies are 2,779 that are going to be allotted.

So, approx **15-16%** of all candidates got selected for the Main Exam and only **3%** will secure their job.

---
## Benefits of Preparing for NORCET?
Preparing for NORCET will also get you prepared for other exams held for nursing staff recruitment as most of their syllabus overlaps.

Following are some other exams for staff nurse recruitment in India.
### National Level Exams
- RRB Staff Nurse Exam (Indian Railways)
- ESIC Nursing Officer Exam
- DSSSB (Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board) Nursing Officer Exam
- SSC (Staff Selection Commission) Staff Nurse Exam
- NIMHANS Staff Nurse Exam
### State Level Exams
- NHM (RSMSSB) - *Rajasthan*
- NHM, UPSSSC, UPPSC, SGPGI, UBTER - *Uttar Pradesh*
- NHM MP - *Madhya Pradesh*
- NHM WB - *West Bengal*
- BTSC - *Bihar*
- HSSC - *Haryana*
- TSPSC/MHSRB - *Telangana*
- And other state specific exams
